Our Story

A family-owned business with a passion for the planet.

Born from a Need for Greener Disposal.

EcoJunk was founded in 2020 by team members who were tired of seeing perfectly good furniture end up in landfill.

Our mission is simple: To provide the most convenient, professional, and sustainable junk removal service in the region. We believe that clutter-free spaces lead to clutter-free minds, and we're here to help you achieve that while protecting our local environment.

"We don't just haul junk; we haul potential. Every item saved from a landfill is a victory for our community."

— Founder, Sarah Green
