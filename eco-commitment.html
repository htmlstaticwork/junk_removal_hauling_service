Sustainability First. Always.

Our mission is to redirect 100% of usable items from landfills. Join us in making a difference.

The Circular Life of Your Junk

Where your items go after they leave your property.

1. Collection

Items are carefully removed and loaded into our eco-friendly trucks.

2. Sorting

Our team sorts every load into four categories: Recycle, Donate, Repurpose, or Landfill.

3. Donation

Usable furniture and clothes go to our network of local charities.

4. Recycling

Metal, paper, plastic, and glass are processed at licensed recycling centers.
